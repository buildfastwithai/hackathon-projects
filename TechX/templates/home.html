Our Key Features

Customize your notes to fit your study style, organizing and formatting them as needed. Plus, with multilingual support, the platform accommodates a wide range of languages.

Audio-to-Text Conversion

Transform spoken words into written notes with ease. Our platform converts lectures, discussions, and any other audio into accurate, searchable text, saving you hours of manual transcription.

Keyword Extraction

Automatically identify and highlight the most important concepts. Our system extracts key terms and ideas from the text, making it easier to focus on what really matters for studying and revision.

Customizable Notes and Summaries

Tailor your notes to your needs. Reorganize, summarize, and format the text, ensuring you capture the information in a way that works best for you.

Multilingual Support

Whether your lecture is in English, Spanish, or any other language, our platform supports a wide range of languages, ensuring students around the world can benefit from this tool.

How To Use

Step 1: Login or Registration

Start by logging into your account or registering if you're a new user.

Step 2: Upload Audio

Upload your audio files in supported formats (MP3, WAV).

Step 3: Keyword Spotting

The system processes your audio to identify relevant keywords and phrases.

Step 4: Generate Notes

The system compiles the keywords into structured notes for you.

Step 6: Download or Share

Download your notes or share them directly through the platform.
